About the club

The Ithaca Creek Swimming Club, known as the Ithaca Sharx, is located in the beautiful grounds of Ithaca Creek State School and warmly welcomes anyone in the local community to come along. The Sharx have a highly professional coaching team provided by the coaches from The Swim Academy. The Sharx offer learn-to-swim lessons and squads, for everyone aged 4-99! We run a friendly Club Night racing competition every Friday evening during the summer terms. Our focus at club nights is beating your own PB and having fun with your mates. We are open all year round and have a heated pool.

More Information

Ithaca Creek Swim Club aka the Ithaca Sharx is a community swimming club, located at Ithaca Creek State School in Red Hill/Bardon in Brisbane's inner west . The heart of the Sharx activities is the Friday club nights during summer, where club swimmers and their families come together for a couple of hours of friendly competition in a positive social environment. We also provide learn to swim (LTS), stroke correction and squad training all year round.

Our Address

We are located within the beautiful grounds of the Ithaca Creek State School, and whilst many of our members are also students at the school, we are a community club with members from the local community as well. The Ithaca Sharx welcomes new members from the school and wider community at any time.
As a Sub-Committee of the Ithaca Creek State School P&C, the success of the Ithaca Creek Swimming Club relies heavily on the efforts of its volunteers. We are constantly seeking extra helpers and always need friendly folk to lend a hand. If you have any skills or time you can offer our committee (big or small, any help gratefully received) please get in touch.
